Black Student Insight

Our Focus

Black Student Insight serves as a platform for current Black college students to share their experiences and beliefs regarding their campuses, while also providing prospective students with general information about the schools, such as overall student diversity demographics, admissions statistics, and tuition for their university of interest.

Meet The Creators

Our Reasoning

The college application process is one of the most stressful periods during a teen’s academic career. Amongst difficult course work, standardized testing, multiple questions probing into their qualifications and life story, high schoolers are constantly being reminded that the decisions made during this time will determine the course of the rest of their lives. Consequently, the pressure to find the “perfect college” drives students to scour the internet searching for videos, articles, and reviews that could provide any insight into what their life would look like on a particular campus. Sites like Niche.com or StudentReviews.com offer students this by allowing students that actually attend the school to rate and review their experiences.

As once prospective high school applicants and now college students, one thing the creators noticed is that it is very difficult for minority students to find insightful information about the cultural communities on these websites. Especially in predominantly white institutions, the minority communities often make up just a small percentage of the school. Despite being a fraction of the overall population, they often have very strong, active communities to the point that the experience of a minority student will be vastly different than that of their white counterparts. Because these communities only make up a small percentage of the school, these experiences are not highly visible on these college rating sites. The sentiments expressed on these websites do not necessarily provide minority prospective students that foresight into what their own lives will be like.
